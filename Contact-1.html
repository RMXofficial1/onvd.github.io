Not so short Biography

Couple of photos to keep you interested:

Hi! My name is Boris Gosevski, and I was born on July 26, 2003, in a town called Kocani in Macedonia. I have lived in Vinica since my birth till the age of four, then I moved to Pescara, Italy and lived there for the next four years
 When I was little, I went to "Slavco Stoimenski" Primary School, and then I went to "Vanco Prke" High School.
 During the summers, I worked as a waiter in different cafes. In 2019, I worked at Gala Cafe, and in 2020, I worked at Angolo Cafe. The next year, in 2021, I worked at Boulevard Cafe for the summer and on weekends. And guess what? In 2022, I even worked in Bulgaria! 
I love learning about computers, and I took some cool courses on IT. I also got my driver's license, finished an English course, and won lots of awards for my traditional dancing. 
When I'm not busy with work and school, I have a lot of fun hobbies. I really like cars, and I'm a big fan! I also enjoy playing sports and computer games. I also love animals and have pets. Oh, and I really want to travel and explore new places with my friends.
One special thing about me is that I volunteered with the Red Cross organization to help people in need. It felt good to make a difference and help others.
In my free time, I used to do kickboxing for a year. It was a great way to stay active and learn self-defense. And for six years, I was part of a folklore assembly. We learned traditional dances and performed together in many countries. 
I'm a unique person with lots of interests and a big heart. I love trying new things, meeting new people, and making the world a better place.

About my town

Vinica is a small town with friendly people and beautiful surroundings. It's full of colorful houses and cozy streets. There are lots of exciting things to see and do. We have a big park and a big town square where people come together for special events and celebrations. We have festivals with music, dancing, foods and drinks. Another cool thing about Vinica is the nature around it. We have beautiful mountains and forests. Living in Vinica makes me feel happy and safe. I have great neighbors who are always friendly and helpful. I'm proud to be a part of Vinica. It's a special place where I can learn, grow, and have fun. I feel lucky to call it my home.
